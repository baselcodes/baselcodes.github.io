About

basel.codes is a collective of coders, artists and designers from basel currently consisting of Ted Davis, Max Frischknecht, Mark Iandovka, Yann Martins, Philipp Möckli, Naz Naddaf, Pedro Neves, Haejeung Paik and Andrea Zaccuri.

We are interested in the creative potential of code and are open, and in solidarity with, people from every gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, race, ethnicity, language, neuro-type, size, ability, class, religion, culture, subculture, political opinion, age, skill level, occupation, and background.
