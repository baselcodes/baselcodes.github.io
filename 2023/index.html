Basel Codes Day – 4th Edition
Saturday, 11.11.2023

For the fourth time, basel.codes cordially invites you to Basel Codes Day (formally PCD). We warmly welcome everyone interested in using code for creative and experimental projects. In the afternoon we’ll have a wide range of talks and workshops followed by A/V performances in the evening.

All talks & workshops are for free!
